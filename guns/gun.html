GUN SAFTY

Never cross a fence, climb a tree or perform any awkward action with a loaded gun. While in the field, there will be times when common sense and the basic rules of firearms safety will require you to unload your gun for maximum safety. Never pull or push a loaded firearm toward yourself or another person.



DIFFERENT TYPES OF GUNS

  • Pistol
  • Rifle
  • Shotgun
  • Snipers

  1. Pistols

    • Small
    • Handheld
    • Small caliber bullets like .45 ACP , 9 mm
    • eg : Glock , Desert Eagle

  2. Rifle

    • Long barrel
    • Accurate shooting
    • Have bolt action or automatic firing

  3. Shotguns

    • Used for hunting
    • Short range
    • Shoots pellets
    • High recoil

  4. Snipers

    • Most Accurate shooting
    • Uses large caliber bullets
    • Long barrels
    • Mainly used by army
